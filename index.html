ABOUT US

M-Farmer is a seed company geared to offering top quality seeds to growers.Founded in 2021, we have progressed to become one of the fastest growing seeds companies in Kenya partnership with leading international plant breeders, innovative research, a robust product development program and a keen understanding of local growing conditions,enable us provide growers with seeds of high quality and distinct added value.This brings enhanced profits and success to our growers and distribution partners.
